Technical Justification

The modernization of the Karachi Circular Railway (KCR) demands rigorous financial evaluation to mitigate the overheads of urban mass transit components operating under elevated environmental strains (coastal salinity, thermal expansion).

This engineering suite directly computes Net Present Value (NPV) of dynamic maintenance lifecycles, applies standardized Capital Recovery Factors (CRF), and models exact Equal Annual Costs (EAC) to objectively identify the most structurally and financially sustainable materials over 10-year, 25-year, and 50-year evaluation horizons.

Continuity Analysis

Foundation Stability
