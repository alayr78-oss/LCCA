System Introduction

Project Objectives & Mission

RailMetric Pro was developed to bridge the gap between structural rail engineering and economic sustainability, providing a mathematically rigorous environment for infrastructure decision-making.

Data-Driven Optimization

Providing a framework for material selection in transit systems like the Karachi Circular Railway (KCR).

Automated LCCA & RCM

Automating complex Life Cycle Cost Analysis and Reliability-Centered Maintenance protocols.

50-Year Horizon Planning

Ensuring fiscal responsibility by projecting long-term viability over half a century of service.

Engineering Perspective

The Engineering Framework

Analyzing core infrastructure components through the lens of structural integrity and economic durability.

Structural Sleepers

Comparative engineering study of Pre-stressed Concrete (PSC) vs. Creosote-Treated Timber, focusing on flexural strength, density, and decay resistance.

Rail Continuity

Technical analysis of Aluminothermic (Thermit) Welding vs. 4-Bolt Fishplate Joints, assessing frequency breakdown and thermal expansion management.

Trackbed Stability

Volumetric assessment of Traditional Ballast Beds (graded crushed stone) vs. Embedded Rail Slab Track (zero-subsidence concrete profiles).

Unique Value Proposition

Project Innovation

Global Versatility

Unlike static models, RailMetric Pro allows users to input localized unit costs and track sizes for any global rail network, from KCR to international standard gauges.

Real-Time Auditing

Every material change triggers an instant update to the NPV and EAC, ensuring zero discrepancies between software logic and manual engineering audits.
